About Barber House

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Barber House has been at the forefront of men's grooming in Casablanca for over 15 years. What started as a small neighborhood barbershop has grown into a premium grooming destination known for excellence and attention to detail.

Our founder, Michael Rodriguez, envisioned a space where men could not only get exceptional grooming services but also relax and enjoy a moment of self-care in a sophisticated environment.

Our Philosophy

At Barber House, we believe that grooming is an essential form of self-expression and self-care. Every haircut, shave, or treatment is an opportunity to enhance not just your appearance but your confidence and well-being.

We combine traditional barbering techniques with modern innovations to deliver services that are both timeless and contemporary.

Our Commitment

  • Premium quality products from trusted brands
  • Continuous training for our barbers in the latest techniques
  • Sterilized and sanitized tools for every client
  • Personalized consultations to understand your unique needs
  • Comfortable, luxurious environment for your relaxation

Hot Towel Shave

The Ultimate Shaving Experience

Experience the luxury and precision of a traditional barber shop shave. Our hot towel shave service is a multi-step process that delivers the closest, most comfortable shave you'll ever have.

Steam Towel

Warm towels to open pores and soften facial hair.

Premium Pre-Shave

Specialized oils to prepare the skin and hair for shaving.

Straight Razor

Traditional straight razor technique for ultimate precision.

Post-Shave Care

Cooling treatments and moisturizers to soothe the skin.

The Process

Our hot towel shave begins with a facial steam treatment to open pores and soften hair. We then apply premium pre-shave oils and create a rich lather with a badger hair brush. Using a straight razor, our barbers perform multiple passes for an exceptionally close shave, finishing with cold compresses and specialized post-shave products to calm and protect your skin.
